Water Conservation Strategies

🚿 Showers: Limit to 5 min, saving ~50L/shower
🔧 Fix leaks: A dripping tap wastes 15L/day
🌿 Garden: Water at dawn to reduce evaporation
👕 Laundry: Run only full loads, saving 30L/cycle
🚽 Toilet: Dual-flush systems cut usage by 40%
🍽️ Dishes: Use a basin rather than running tap
